## Project Overview
**Food4Need** is a web and mobile-accessible platform designed to **connect food donors** (restaurants, grocery stores, bakeries, and households) with **local food banks and shelters** in real-time.  

The primary goals of the application are:  
- Reduce food waste across communities in Ontario.  
- Improve the efficiency of food distribution.  
- Support and strengthen social-benefiting organizations.  

---

## Features
- Donors can **post available food donations** with details such as type, quantity, and expiry date.  
- Shelters can **view and claim donations** in real-time.  
- Admin can **approve donations and registrations**, manage events, and oversee the system.  
- **Notifications** for food expiry and donation approvals.  
- **Live map view** to locate nearby donors and shelters.  
- **Feedback and review** system for donors and shelters.
